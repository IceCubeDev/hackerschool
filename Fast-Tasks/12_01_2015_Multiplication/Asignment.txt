A sequence of the squares of subsequent natural numbers is 1491625364964....
Write a program multiplication which tells you the gidit located at a fixed position in the sequence.

Input
single integer N - the number of the digit in the sequence.

Output
outputs the digit located at the given position

Restrictions: 0 < n < 3200000

EXAMPLE
Input: 6
Output: 2
---
Input: 10
Output: 4
